Calling all local artists!

One important value of Racy D'Lene's is supporting the local art and music scene in the Chippewa Valley! We welcome all local artists to share their art with us here, whether that be putting up posters for an upcoming show, doing an art installation on our classic brick wall, or selling fun stickers at the register. Feel free to drop in or call us at 715-834-0000 to learn more about how we can help you promote your craft!
